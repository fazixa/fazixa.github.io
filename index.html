    No_Fire

    In a group of nine, we participated in a challange proposed by NASA and I worked as a devoloper and a designer to build No_fire application. No_Fire is an interactive service built under Dr.Pourreza’s supervision. Its main purpose is to predict and estimate the probability of fire existence in a worldwide scale by image and text processing on social media. It also analyzes tweets using Natural Language Processing techniques and employs NASA APIs to alarm the people near the incident.
